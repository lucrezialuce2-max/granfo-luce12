Our Story

Artisans of light since always.

Granfo Luce was born in 1970 from a passion for light and design. For over fifty years, we have created lighting pieces that blend aesthetics, functionality, and artisanal care, bringing light and style to diverse environments. Our company is deeply rooted in tradition while staying attentive to the evolution of taste and living needs. Every creation is the result of craftsmanship passed down over time, fused with the pursuit of new forms and quality materials. Granfo Luce stands out for manufacturing excellence, product customization, and a philosophy that places the beauty of light as a furnishing element at the center. For us, lighting is not just a technical matter: it is a way to tell stories, create atmospheres, and enhance spaces.
