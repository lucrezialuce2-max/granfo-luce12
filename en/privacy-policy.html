Privacy Notice

This Privacy Policy describes how GRANFO ILLUMINAZIONE S.N.C. ("Granfo Luce" or "we") collects, uses, and protects users' personal data on the website granfoluce.it in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR).

2. Data Collected

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Contact data: name, surname, email, phone number (provided via contact or newsletter forms)
  • Browsing data: IP address, browser used, pages visited, time spent on the site (automatically collected through technical cookies)
  • Communication data: messages sent via contact form or quote requests

3. Purpose of Processing

Personal data are processed for the following purposes:

  • Responses to requests: to manage information requests, quotes, and customer support
  • Newsletter: to send updates on new products, catalogs, and promotions (only with your explicit consent)
  • Service improvement: to analyze website usage and improve user experience
  • Legal obligations: to comply with regulatory and tax obligations

4. Legal Basis

Processing of personal data is based on:

  • Consent: for sending marketing communications (newsletter)
  • Performance of a contract: for managing orders and quotes
  • Legitimate interest: for analytical and service improvement purposes
  • Legal obligation: for tax and accounting compliance

5. Data Recipients

Your personal data may be communicated to:

  • Granfo Luce collaborators and employees authorized for processing
  • Technical service providers (hosting, email marketing) as processors
  • Tax consultants and accountants for accounting compliance
  • Public authorities in case of legal obligation

Data are not sold to third parties for commercial purposes without your explicit consent.

6. Data Retention

Personal data are retained for the time strictly necessary for their purposes:

  • Contact requests: up to 24 months from the request
  • Newsletter: until consent is revoked
  • Accounting data: 10 years for tax obligations

7. Data Subject Rights

In accordance with the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: obtain confirmation of your data and receive a copy
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: request deletion of data (right to be forgotten)
  • Restriction: restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Portability: receive data in a structured format and transfer them to another controller
  • Objection: object to processing for legitimate reasons
  • Withdrawal of consent: withdraw consent at any time (e.g., for newsletter)

To exercise your rights, contact us at: granfoluce@hotmail.it

8. Cookies

The site uses technical cookies necessary for its operation (e.g., saving preferences). We do not use profiling or third-party tracking cookies without your consent.

You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.

9. Data Security

We adopt appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or alteration. The site uses secure connections (HTTPS), and data are stored on protected servers.

10. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be published on this page with the update date. Please check back periodically.

Last update: December 19, 2025

11. Complaints

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority in your country in case of privacy violations.
